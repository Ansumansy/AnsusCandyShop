About Us

Ansu's Candy Shop was started because the lines in the cafeteria where to long. This caused many people to go hungry so I started the shop. 30 percent of the profits will be going to a local charity.

Welcome to Ansu's Candy Shop! We are a small, family-owned candy shop located in the heart of the city. Our passion for sweets and love for our community is what inspired us to open our doors and share our sweets with the world. Our shop is dedicated to providing the finest, freshest, and most delicious candies available. We believe that everyone deserves a sweet treat every now and then, and it is our mission to bring joy to our customers with every bite. Whether you're a chocolate lover, a gummy fan, or just have a sweet tooth, we've got something for you. At Ansu's Candy Shop, we take pride in our handcrafted sweets and carefully source only the best ingredients. From our rich and creamy chocolates, to our chewy and fruity gummies, every bite is a taste of pure heaven. Our store is more than just a place to buy candy, it's a destination for those looking for a sweet escape. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is always on hand to help you find the perfect treat, and our warm and inviting atmosphere will make you feel right at home. So, come visit us at Ansu's Candy Shop and taste the difference that only a small, family-owned candy shop can offer. We can't wait to share our sweet creations with you!
